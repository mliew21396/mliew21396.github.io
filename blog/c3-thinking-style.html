Learning Struggles and My Action Plan

June 28, 2015

My time with Phase 0 so far has been good. I'm picking up the material pretty well and not getting too caught up with problems.

This post will cover my learning styles and see if I can pinpoint where to improve with it. My learning style is split between two Gregorc thinking styles: abstract sequential and abstract random. Abstract Random Thinkers organize information through reflection, and thrive in unstructured, people-oriented environments. Abstract Sequential Thinkers love the world of theory and abstract thought.

If I were to pinpoint my biggest struggle, it'd be staying focused reading texts and documentation. It just seems pretty dull. Mixing it with exercises helped though.

To counteract this, I've tried timeblocking with varying results. I've isolated myself with a timer at the library and that seemed to help though sometimes I can get distracted. I will try some more blocking intervals to see if they work better.

The other conversation I want to cover is Growth Mindset vs Fixed Mindset. While I feel I have a strong Growth Mindset, I can improve in a few areas. Specifically, I want to focus on persisting in the face of setbacks. If I struggle with a concept and don't find a solution within and hour, I get discouraged. Retraining my mind to think I can always come back tomorrow and look at topics in a fresh light will be key to developing this mindset's aspect.
